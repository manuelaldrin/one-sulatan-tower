Welcome to One Sulatan Tower

Located in the hustle bustle stretch of Mandaluyong, One Sulatan is nested in the emerging retail strip in Sulatan street corner Barangka Avenue. The 5-storey building is expected to boost urban living as it rises among various fast food chains, pharmacy, and the renowned Rizal Technology University.​

  • Fitted Handover Condition: with A/C, Tile ​flooring, Finished Ceiling with Lights.
  • Exclusive restroom provided per unit.

  • Tenant privacy with just maximum of 4 ​tenants per floor​
  • Open balcony hallways
